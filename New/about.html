Our Story

Founded in 2025, we are a startup company by 5 individuals with one and only motivation: to give students, undergraduates, graduates and even electricians a reliable online shop to get their hands on the most cost-efficiently electrical components they can.

Despite being a small and sketchy online shop, we wish to improve ourselves by receiving responses from our customers to upgrade both quantitives and qualities of our products as well as deliver more updates to the web in near future.
